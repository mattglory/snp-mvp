## â“ Common Deposit Questions

### Q: How long does a deposit take?
**A:** Typically 10-20 seconds for transaction confirmation, then your tokens are immediately earning yield.

### Q: Can I deposit Bitcoin (BTC) directly?
**A:** Currently, you need to deposit STX or sBTC. Direct BTC deposits coming in Phase 2 via sBTC bridge.

### Q: What happens to my tokens after I deposit?
**A:** Your STX is automatically deployed across 12+ yield strategies based on your vault's allocation. You receive vault tokens (snSTX) representing your share.

### Q: Can I change vaults after depositing?
**A:** Yes! Withdraw from one vault and deposit into another. Or keep positions in multiple vaults simultaneously.

### Q: Is there a maximum deposit amount?
**A:** No hard maximum, but very large deposits (>100K STX) may need multiple transactions for gas optimization.

### Q: Do I need to claim rewards?
**A:** No! All rewards are automatically harvested, compounded, and reflected in your vault token value. Just deposit and watch it grow.

### Q: Can I lose money?
**A:** Yes. While SNP aims to generate positive returns, DeFi involves risks including smart contract bugs, protocol exploits, and market volatility. Never deposit more than you can afford to lose.
