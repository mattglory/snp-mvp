### How Fees Are Calculated

```clarity
;; Simplified fee calculation logic

(define-constant PERFORMANCE_FEE u150)  ;; 1.5% = 150 basis points

(define-public (charge-performance-fee (profit uint))
  (let
    (
      (fee-amount (/ (* profit PERFORMANCE_FEE) u10000))
      (protocol-share (/ (* fee-amount u33) u100))     ;; 33% of fee
      (strategist-share (/ (* fee-amount u20) u100))   ;; 20% of fee
      (dao-share (/ (* fee-amount u47) u100))          ;; 47% of fee
    )
    
    ;; Distribute fees
    (try! (distribute-fee protocol-share .snp-treasury))
    (try! (distribute-fee strategist-share (get-strategist)))
    (try! (distribute-fee dao-share .snp-dao))
    
    (ok {
      total-fee: fee-amount,
      user-keeps: (- profit fee-amount)
    })
  )
)
```

## â“ Fee FAQ

### Q: When exactly are fees charged?
**A:** During harvest cycles (every 24-72 hours) when profits are realized and compounded. You'll see fee deduction in your transaction history.

### Q: Do I pay fees on my initial deposit?
**A:** No. Zero deposit fees. You deposit 1,000 STX, the full 1,000 STX goes to work.

### Q: What if a strategy loses money?
**A:** No fee charged. Performance fees are only on profits, never on losses or principal.

### Q: Are fees tax deductible?
**A:** Possibly, depending on your jurisdiction. Consult a tax professional. SNP provides transaction history for tax purposes.

### Q: Can fees be paid in a different token?
**A:** No. Fees are deducted from the earned yield in the same asset (STX or sBTC).

### Q: How do SNP's fees compare to TradFi?
**A:** Traditional fund managers typically charge 2% management + 20% performance = much higher. SNP is 0% management + 1.5% performance.

### Q: Do I see gross or net APY on the dashboard?
**A:** NET APY after fees. What you see is what you get. Unlike competitors who show gross APY.

### Q: What are gas fees used for?
**A:** Gas fees go to Stacks validators who process transactions. SNP doesn't receive gas fees.

### Q: Can I avoid the performance fee?
**A:** No. It's automatic and built into the smart contracts. But at 1.5%, it's among the lowest in DeFi.

### Q: Why is the fee split three ways?
**A:** 
- Protocol (0.5%): Operations, development, security
- Strategist (0.3%): Incentivize strategy creation
- DAO (0.7%): Community treasury, grants, buybacks

This ensures sustainable ecosystem growth.
